Inlaks Energy is a subsidiary of Inlaks Nigeria in partnership with the leading OEMs in the technology industry to provide world class IT and Infrastructure Solutions that exceed the needs of customers. Our customers cuts across various segments including Banking, Telecommunication, Oil/Gas, Power, Manufacturing, Utility and the Distribution sectors. Our Customer Service Centers are equipped with first class and modern state of the art equipment to cater for installation and repair works for all our unique brand of products which also forms the basis of our business operations. We have a team of excellent Customer Service Professionals 24/7 to support your interest whenever the need arises.

Inlaks Energy pride itself in being the home inverter solution bus stop and its world class design and installation have over the years stood out from competitors.

Our expertise in power systems and in alternative energy provides us with the knowledge to develop solutions that answer the needs of today’s clients. These solutions allow our clients to:

  • Live smartly and happily.
  • Reduce the operating costs which includes cost of ownership of home inverter solution.
  • Increase customer satisfaction and repeat sales
